Welcome to SiberConsulting


SiberConsulting is a 15-year-old Boston based professional services firm that provides a broad range of industry and strategic advisory services. By using our proprietary models and frameworks, as well as our senior level executive relationships that transcend decades, we help our clients get to the finish line bigger and faster. We have expertise in the telecom and utility, hospitality, financial services, healthcare and transportation verticals.



Illustrative engagement examples include:

  • new market entry
  • new product launch
  • pricing models
  • rebranding
  • merges & acquisitions
  • roll-up strategy
  • monetizing existing data
  • roll-up strategy
  • comprehensive customer shape & insights


We welcome preliminary calls and emails to discuss how we can add value and help you to meet or exceed your business objectives.

Meet Richard Siber

Founder of SiberConsulting

Richard is a proven senior executive with a 35+ year record of achievement in leadership, business management, and professional services. The majority of his career has been spent in the telecommunications industry, specializing in all things mobile and wireless. Richard has an established track record of managing C suite relationships and driving strategy and measurable performance improvements.

As a recognized global leader and pioneer, Richard has impacted the wireless ecosystem for decades. Currently, Richard has shifted his focus to emerging technologies at the intersections of big data, data analytics, data sciences and IOT.

Richard is a sought after speaker and frequent contributor on some of the largest platforms, including The Wall Street Journal, Financial Times of London, Forbes, Fortune, Business Week, NBC, CNBC and the Huffington Post.

Since founding SiberConsulting, Richard has sat on 19 boards of technology companies, both public and private. He has served on all committees, frequently chairing the audit committee.

Richard earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from Boston University, a Masters Degree in Business Administration from Boston College, and holds a certificate of special studies from Harvard Extension School. He also taught at the Carroll School of Management at Boston College for 10 years.

When asked about his biggest accomplishments, Richard will joyously share that being a dad is the most fulfilling and rewarding accomplishment of all. He takes great pride in his two children, both Summa Cum Laude graduates who have joined startups in fintech and healthcare.

For fun, Richard enjoys working outside, playing ice hockey, eating the foods of his Middle Eastern heritage, reading anything by Brad Thor, and spending time with his family.
